luckyxlove pride capsule collection

In 2019, I had entered a shirt design contest held by Lucky Brand in honor of Pride. My shirt design had won and was sold nationwide online and at Lucky Brand stores for their Pride Summer Capsule Collection in honor of the 50th anniversary of World Pride. Over $10,000 of the proceeds was donated to the LA LGBT Center

speck x verizon x builtbygirls phone case

As one of the two winners of the #BUILTBYGIRLS Phone Case Design winner, my phone case design will be sold nationwide in Verizon stores and online, produced by Speck starting in the Fall 2019. The design is in representation of #BUILTBYGIRLS mission and an embodiement of encouraging women to not only fight for their seat at the table, but to build their own table.
